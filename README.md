## 📖 About The Project

This website was built to showcase the fleet, services, and team of **Betty Penny Car Rental**. It allows potential customers to:

- Browse available vehicles (Land Cruiser, Jeep, Nissan, Minibus, etc.)
- View pricing options (hourly, daily, monthly rates)
- Learn about additional services (wedding ceremonies, city tours, travel consulting)
- Meet the company’s management team
- Read recent blog posts
- Contact the company via a built-in form

The site is **fully static** (HTML/CSS/JS) and can be easily customised for any car rental business.

## 🛠 Built With

- **HTML5** – semantic structure
- **CSS3 / SCSS** – custom styling (compiled via Prepros)
- **Bootstrap 4** – grid system, components, utilities
- **jQuery** – DOM manipulation and plugins
- **Owl Carousel** – testimonials and featured vehicles carousel
- **Animate.css / AOS** – scroll animations
- **Google Fonts** – Poppins font
- **Flaticon / Icomoon / Ionicons** – icon packs
- **Prepros** – SCSS compilation and project management (see `prepros-6.config`)
